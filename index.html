My name is Mai Banh and I am living in the Bay Area. I am a first-generation Vietnames-American. I was a dental assistant and had the full intention of going to dental school and try to become a dentist; however, after looking closer at the commitment, I decided that it wasn't for me.
During my time in the dentistry field, I did get exposed to an amazing application that allowed oral surgeon to plan their surgery using 3D rendering. I realized at that point that I wanted to be a part of a team that bridge technology and health care.
I am now attending a full stack web developement bootcamp with the goal of improving my knowledge of web development.
